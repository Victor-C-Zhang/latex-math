\section{}
Recall $y$ has order 2, and $x$ has order 8. Then repeated application of the identity $yx = x^{-1}y$ yields
\begin{equation*}
\begin{split}
x^4y^3x^{-4}yxy &= x^4yx^{-4}x^{-1}\\
&= x^4yx^3\\
&= x^4x^{-3}y\\
&= xy \; \Box
\end{split}
\end{equation*}
